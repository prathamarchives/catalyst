# 03 — extract Catalyst Brain

Extract identity + judgment + standards + adaptive feedback from approved sources and interview answers.

For every significant claim:

```txt
claim:
status: observed | assumed | confirmed | retired
evidence:
affected files:
affected tasks:
```

Route signal into the correct file. Do not dump everything into feedback-memory.

Extraction rules:

- behavior across sessions outranks self-description
- user corrections outrank generic style preferences
- rejected examples outrank banned-word lists
- standards must become reviewable criteria
- judgment must become action rules
- voice is one layer, not the product
- assumptions go to open-questions until confirmed
