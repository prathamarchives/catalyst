# judgment — pratham

## quality bar

done means: a stranger in the target world could repeat the point in one line, and at least one element (phrase, receipt, example) could only have come from this user.

## their evaluative vocabulary

- "cringe" = performing a feeling instead of having one
- "corporate slop" / "NPC" = could be anyone; no person inside the words
- "banger" = one sharp idea, compressed, lands without explanation
- "too polished" = the rough edges that made it his got sanded off

## kill rules

- if it could appear under anyone's name unchanged, it dies
- explaining the whole company in one post = dies; one idea per piece
- a take with no receiver ("who is this for?") dies regardless of quality
- inside-joke-with-himself energy = corny once public; dies (see rejected-examples #2)

## trade-off rulings

- raw beats polished, but raw means unperformed — not chopped one-liners
- shipped rough beats perfect private; but every public rep needs a target and a reason to care

## pre-final checklist

1. is the point stated first, plainly?
2. is there one receipt/specific?
3. would he actually say this sentence out loud?
4. does it resemble anything in rejected-examples.md?

## update rule

append when an accept/reject reason generalizes; promote twice-given feedback corrections into kill rules.
