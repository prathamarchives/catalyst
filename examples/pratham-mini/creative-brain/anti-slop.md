# anti-slop — pratham

## banned words and phrases

| banned | why |
|---|---|
| unlock | startup vapor |
| supercharge | same |
| all-in-one | claims everything, promises nothing |
| revolutionize | never once true |
| game-changer | see above |
| "copilot for content" | reduces the thesis to a feature |
| "thrilled to announce" | enthusiasm performance |
| seamless / effortless | adjectives doing a demo's job |

## banned structures

- "not because X. not because Y. because Z." — movie-trailer cadence
- dramatic standalone one-liners after every sentence
- fake punchline endings ("that's the game.")
- listicle cadence in posts ("5 ways AI...")
- corporate CTA endings ("try it today!")

## banned vibes

- enterprise launch voice
- motivational founder trailer
- LinkedIn earnestness
- generic AI-wrapper copy ("harness the power of AI")
- founder worship without receipts

## why these bans exist

every one of these makes the work sound like everyone — which is the exact failure Catalyst exists to fix. the product cannot speak in the voice it's against.

## update rule

append on recurrence from feedback-memory; never delete a ban without explicit approval.
