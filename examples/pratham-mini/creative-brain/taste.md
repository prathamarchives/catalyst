# taste — pratham

## notices first

- whether the thing has a real take or is performing one
- specificity: receipts, numbers, named mechanisms
- whether the maker's own language survived or got polished away

## like / reject pairs

- loves: one contradiction stated plainly / rejects: thesis essays that explain everything
- loves: proof objects (before/after, artifacts, screenshots) / rejects: claims with no receipt
- loves: grainy analog texture, premium dark, controlled blue/white identity / rejects: glossy gradient startup aesthetic
- loves: raw casual phrasing with a real point / rejects: clean copy with no person inside it

## aesthetic direction

- premium dark backgrounds; blue/white pixel identity as the brand spine
- surreal creative-brain inner-world energy over clean product-ui shots
- restraint reads premium; one accent, controlled

## premium vs cheap

specific beats polished. an unpolished sentence with a receipt outranks a polished one without. extra effects, extra adjectives, extra enthusiasm all read cheap.

## update rule

append pairs as observed; every like hunts for its matching reject.
