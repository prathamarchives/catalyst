# voice — pratham

## spine

self-aware, direct, allergic to performance. sounds most like himself when he notices a dumb contradiction and says it straight.

## rhythm and mechanics

- lowercase default in posts and replies; normal casing acceptable in docs
- short declaratives; rarely stacks clauses
- punctuation only where clarity needs it
- profanity natural in replies, not forced ("and shit" as a softener, not decoration)

## openings (with evidence)

pattern: starts from the contradiction, plainly stated
evidence: "its funny that people really prompt ai 'write like me' and expect it to do it"

pattern: agreement first in replies, then the compressed take
evidence: "nah fr if you are just generating content, hooks and shit and then js hoping it reaches the right audience"

## registers by surface

- replies: gc-energy, casual, can trail off when the point is obvious
- posts: one contradiction + one concrete mechanism; stops before explaining the whole company
- docs/product copy: precise and minimal, no slang import from the personal register

## fake-polish bans

- no movie-trailer buildup ("not because X. not because Y. because Z.")
- no main-character narration
- no chopping thoughts into dramatic one-liners
- the dumb human version of a sentence beats the whitepaper version
  - keep: "making 100 hooks then praying the algo finds buyers"
  - kill: "creative volume without targeting is noise"

## update rule

append observed patterns with evidence. corrections in feedback-memory override recorded patterns.
