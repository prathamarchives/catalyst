# identity.md — template

## purpose

Who this user/project is at the level that changes creative decisions: what they're building, what world they operate in, and — just as important — what they are not.

## what belongs here

- one-paragraph self-description in (or close to) the user's own words
- what they're building/making and why it matters to them
- the worlds/scenes they orbit (their audience lives here too)
- their edge: what they bring that others in their lane don't
- explicit "what I am NOT" list — the mistaken identities that annoy them

## what does not belong here

- biography trivia that never affects creative output
- current projects and goals (that's context.md — identity is slow-moving)
- voice mechanics (voice.md)

## suggested sections

```md
## who
## building
## worlds
## edge
## not
```

## example entries

```md
## who
independent game developer, ten years in, solo by choice. teaches while building.

## not
- not an "indie hustle" guy — hates monetization-first framing
- not a tutorial channel — teaching happens through real devlogs, not lessons
```

## update rule

Slow file. Update only when the user's self-description or direction genuinely shifts (confirmed by them), not per-session. Date major changes.
